# Red Team Operations Planning Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Red Team Operations Planning Brainstorming Skill specialises in comprehensive adversary simulation strategy and tactical planning for realistic security testing operations. This skill empowers strategic thinking through systematic threat modelling, attack tree development, and operational security planning for simulated advanced persistent threat (APT) scenarios and sophisticated attack campaigns.

## Core Capabilities

### Adversary Simulation Strategy Framework
- **Threat Actor Profiling and Emulation**: Strategic analysis and simulation of real-world threat actor tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) based on intelligence-driven threat modelling
- **MITRE ATT&CK Framework Integration**: Comprehensive mapping of attack strategies using the MITRE ATT&CK framework for systematic adversary behaviour simulation
- **Attack Tree Development**: Strategic decomposition of complex attack objectives into executable tactical operations with logical dependency mapping

### Red Team Operation Design
- **Mission Objective Definition**: Clear articulation of red team engagement goals including specific security control testing and organisational readiness assessment
- **Rules of Engagement Development**: Comprehensive operational boundaries including authorised tactics, protected systems, and escalation procedures
- **Timeline and Resource Planning**: Strategic allocation of operational phases, team resources, and engagement duration for maximum assessment effectiveness

### Operational Security and Stealth Planning
- **OPSEC Integration Strategy**: Comprehensive operational security planning to maintain engagement realism while protecting red team activities
- **Detection Evasion Planning**: Strategic analysis of defensive capabilities and development of evasion techniques for realistic adversary simulation
- **Communication Security**: Secure command and control planning including encrypted communications and operational compartmentalisation

## Implementation Framework

### Phase 1: Threat Intelligence and Objective Setting (75 minutes)
- Conduct comprehensive threat landscape analysis relevant to the target organisation including industry-specific threat actors
- Define clear engagement objectives including specific security controls to test and organisational capabilities to assess
- Select appropriate threat actor profile for emulation based on organisational risk assessment and intelligence requirements

### Phase 2: Attack Strategy Development (90 minutes)
- Develop comprehensive attack tree mapping primary objectives to specific tactical operations and intermediate goals
- Map selected tactics, techniques, and procedures to MITRE ATT&CK framework for systematic coverage and documentation
- Design multi-phase operation plan including initial access, persistence, lateral movement, and objective achievement strategies

### Phase 3: Operational Planning and Risk Assessment (85 minutes)
- Establish comprehensive rules of engagement including authorised tactics, system boundaries, and safety protocols
- Develop operational security plan including detection evasion, communication security, and evidence management
- Create detailed timeline and resource allocation plan with contingency procedures and abort criteria

## Key Techniques and Methodologies

### Strategic Planning Methodologies
- **Intelligence-Driven Planning**: Base operation planning on actual threat intelligence and real-world adversary behaviour patterns
- **Risk-Based Approach**: Prioritise testing efforts based on organisational risk tolerance and business impact assessment
- **Collaborative Planning**: Integrate multiple red team disciplines including social engineering, technical exploitation, and physical security assessment

### Threat Actor Emulation
- **APT Group Behaviour Modeling**: Detailed emulation of specific advanced persistent threat groups including their tools, techniques, and operational patterns
- **Industry-Specific Threat Simulation**: Focus on threat actors relevant to the target organisation's industry sector and threat landscape
- **Campaign Lifecycle Simulation**: Model complete attack campaigns from initial reconnaissance through objective achievement and persistence

### Operation Security and Ethics
- **Minimal Impact Operations**: Design operations that achieve testing objectives while minimising business disruption and operational impact
- **Evidence Management**: Comprehensive planning for operation documentation, evidence collection, and post-engagement cleanup
- **Legal and Compliance Integration**: Ensure all planned activities comply with legal requirements and organisational policies
