# Advanced Pricing Strategy - Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose
Develop sophisticated pricing strategies through systematic brainstorming that integrates behavioral economics, competitive dynamics, value engineering, and multi-dimensional pricing models.

## Core Capabilities

### Pricing Psychology Integration
- **Behavioral Economics Framework**: Apply cognitive biases and psychological triggers to pricing decisions
- **Value Perception Engineering**: Design pricing to enhance perceived value and willingness to pay
- **Choice Architecture**: Structure pricing options to guide customer decision-making
- **Anchoring Strategies**: Leverage reference points and price anchoring effects

- **Temporal Demand Patterns**: Model pricing based on time-of-day, seasonal, and cyclical factors
- **Lifecycle Pricing**: optimise prices across product/service lifecycle stages
- **Event-Driven Pricing**: Reactive pricing for market events, competitors, and external factors

#### Customer-Centric Models
- **Segment-Based Pricing**: Develop pricing strategies for distinct customer segments
- **Usage-Based Pricing**: Create tiered models based on consumption patterns
- **Value-Based Pricing**: Align pricing with customer-specific value realization
- **Personalized Pricing**: Individual pricing optimisation using customer data
