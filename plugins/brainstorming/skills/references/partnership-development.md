# Partnership Development Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Partnership Development Brainstorming Skill specialises in strategic alliance creation, collaborative relationship management, and systematic partnership ecosystem development. This skill empowers strategic thinking through partner identification, value alignment assessment, and the creation of mutually beneficial partnerships that extend capabilities, expand market reach, and create competitive advantages through collaborative value creation.

## Core Capabilities

### Partnership Strategy and Opportunity Assessment
- **Strategic Partnership Identification and Evaluation**: Systematically identify and evaluate potential partners including capability assessment, strategic alignment, and mutual value potential
- **Partnership Portfolio and Ecosystem Design**: Create comprehensive partnership ecosystems including different partnership types, roles, and strategic objectives
- **Value Proposition and Mutual Benefit Analysis**: Analyse partnership value propositions ensuring mutual benefit and sustainable collaboration

### Partner Due Diligence and Selection
- **Partner Assessment and Due Diligence**: Conduct comprehensive partner evaluation including financial stability, cultural alignment, and strategic capability assessment
- **Risk Assessment and Mitigation Planning**: Identify partnership risks including dependency, competitive, and operational risks with appropriate mitigation strategies
- **Cultural Compatibility and Integration Planning**: Assess organisational culture compatibility and plan integration approaches supporting successful collaboration

### Partnership Structuring and Management
- **Partnership Agreement and Governance Structure**: Design partnership agreements including governance, decision-making, and conflict resolution mechanisms
- **Performance Management and KPI Framework**: Create partnership performance measurement including success metrics, milestone tracking, and continuous improvement
- **Relationship Management and Communication**: Implement systematic relationship management including communication protocols and stakeholder engagement

## Implementation Framework

### Partnership Development Process

**Phase 1: Partnership Strategy and Opportunity Analysis (120 minutes)**
- Define partnership strategy including strategic objectives, capability gaps, and partnership priorities aligned with business strategy
- Conduct market analysis and partner landscape assessment identifying potential partners and competitive dynamics
- Analyse internal capabilities and resources determining partnership requirements and value contribution potential
- Create partnership framework including partnership types, selection criteria, and governance models

**Phase 2: Partner Identification and Due Diligence (150 minutes)**
- Identify potential partners using systematic partner research and network analysis
- Conduct comprehensive due diligence including financial assessment, capability evaluation, and strategic fit analysis
- Assess cultural compatibility and integration requirements including organisational alignment and collaboration potential
- Develop partnership business case including value proposition, investment requirements, and expected returns

**Phase 3: Partnership Negotiation and Implementation (90 minutes)**
- Negotiate partnership agreements including terms, governance structure, and performance expectations
- Implement partnership including integration planning, communication systems, and collaborative workflows
- Establish performance monitoring and management systems tracking partnership success and identifying improvement opportunities
- Create ongoing relationship management processes ensuring partnership sustainability and value optimisation
