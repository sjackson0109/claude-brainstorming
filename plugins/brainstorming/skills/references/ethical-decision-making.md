# Ethical Decision Making Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Ethical Decision Making Brainstorming Skill specialises in developing systematic approaches to navigating complex moral and ethical challenges whilst creating frameworks that ensure responsible business practices, stakeholder consideration, and long-term value creation. This skill empowers strategic thinking through ethical analysis, moral reasoning, and the creation of comprehensive frameworks that balance competing interests whilst maintaining integrity and building sustainable trust.

## Core Capabilities

### Ethical Framework Development and Moral Analysis
- **Ethical Theory Application and Moral Reasoning**: Apply ethical frameworks including deontological ethics, consequentialism, virtue ethics, and care ethics for comprehensive moral analysis
- **Stakeholder Impact Assessment and Consideration**: Evaluate stakeholder implications including impact analysis, stakeholder prioritisation, and ethical obligation assessment
- **Value Conflict Resolution and Priority Setting**: Navigate value conflicts including competing priorities, ethical dilemma resolution, and value hierarchy establishment

### Ethical Risk Assessment and Compliance Integration
- **Ethical Risk Identification and Impact Analysis**: Identify ethical risks including reputational risks, compliance violations, and moral hazard assessment
- **Regulatory Compliance and Legal Framework Integration**: Integrate compliance including regulatory requirements, legal obligations, and ethical standard alignment
- **Corporate Governance and Accountability Framework**: Establish governance including ethical oversight, accountability mechanisms, and transparency requirements

### Ethical Culture Development and Implementation
- **Ethical Culture Building and Value Integration**: Build ethical culture including value articulation, behaviour modeling, and ethical norm establishment
- **Ethical Training and Awareness Development**: Implement training including ethics education, awareness building, and decision-making skill development
- **Ethical Communication and Stakeholder Engagement**: Create communication including transparent dialogue, stakeholder engagement, and ethical disclosure

## Implementation Framework

### Ethical Decision Making Development Process

**Phase 1: Ethical Context Analysis and Framework Establishment (120 minutes)**
- Conduct ethical landscape assessment including ethical challenges identification, stakeholder mapping, and value system analysis
- Establish ethical framework including moral principles, decision-making criteria, and ethical standard definition
- Analyse organisational values including value clarification, ethical culture assessment, and integrity baseline establishment
- Create ethical governance including oversight structure, accountability mechanisms, and decision-making authority

**Phase 2: Ethical Analysis and Decision Support Development (150 minutes)**
- Develop ethical decision-making process including systematic analysis, stakeholder consultation, and ethical evaluation criteria
- Create ethical dilemma resolution framework including conflict analysis, alternative evaluation, and solution development
- Implement stakeholder consideration including impact assessment, rights evaluation, and obligation analysis
- Design ethical risk management including risk identification, mitigation strategies, and monitoring systems

**Phase 3: Implementation and Ethical Culture Integration (90 minutes)**
- Execute ethical integration including policy implementation, training delivery, and cultural reinforcement
- Establish ethical monitoring including compliance tracking, ethical behaviour assessment, and culture measurement
- Create feedback and improvement systems including ethical feedback, learning integration, and continuous enhancement
- Design communication and transparency including stakeholder communication, ethical reporting, and transparent disclosure
