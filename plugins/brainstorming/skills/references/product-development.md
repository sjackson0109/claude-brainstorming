# Product Development Brainstorming

## Activation Triggers

This skill responds to product development brainstorming requests:

- "Brainstorm product roadmap for [timeframe]"
- "Help me brainstorm feature prioritisation for [product]"
- "Let's brainstorm MVP design for [concept]"
- "Brainstorm product strategy for [market]"
- "Help me brainstorm user experience for [feature]"
- "Let's brainstorm development methodology for [team]"
- "Brainstorm product lifecycle planning"
- "Help me brainstorm R&D strategy"

## Scope

**What this covers:**
- Product vision and strategic positioning
- Roadmap planning and feature prioritisation
- MVP definition and validation strategies
- User experience and design thinking
- Development methodology and process design
- Product lifecycle management
- Innovation pipeline and R&D planning

**What this doesn't cover:**
- Technical architecture decisions (use Technical Architecture Brainstorming)
- Marketing and go-to-market strategy (use Commercial Strategy Brainstorming)
- Internal team processes (use Business Operations Brainstorming)
- Long-term market strategy (use Strategic Planning Brainstorming)

## Conversation Rules

1. **One question at a time** - Focus on specific product decisions
2. **Multiple choice preferred** - Present options for easier decision-making
3. **Lead with recommendation** - Propose 2-3 approaches, recommend one with reasoning
4. **User-centric focus** - Always start with user needs and value delivery
5. **Validate incrementally** - Present planning sections, check alignment before continuing

## Process

**Understanding the product opportunity:**
- Current product state and user feedback
- User needs, pain points, and desired outcomes
- Competitive landscape and differentiation opportunities
- Business objectives and success metrics

**Exploring development approaches:**
- Propose 2-3 different strategies with trade-offs
- Consider team capabilities, timeline, and resource constraints
- Evaluate user impact and business value

**Presenting the plan:**
- Break into logical phases with clear milestones
- Focus on user value and business impact
- Validate product-market alignment after each section

## Key Frameworks

**Product Strategy Questions:**
- "What's your product vision and how does it serve users?"
- "Who are your target users and what jobs are they hiring your product to do?"
- "How will you measure product success and user satisfaction?"
- "What's your competitive advantage and sustainable differentiation?"

**Development Planning:**
- Feature prioritisation methods (RICE, MoSCoW, Value vs Effort)
- Development methodology (Agile, Scrum, Kanban, hybrid)
- User research and validation approaches
- Quality assurance and testing strategies

**Output Focus:**
- Clear product vision and user value proposition
- Prioritised feature roadmap with rationale
- Practical development approach and methodology
- Measurable success criteria and user metrics
