# Scenario Modelling Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Scenario Modelling Brainstorming Skill specialises in systematic development, analysis, and application of alternative future scenarios to support strategic planning, risk assessment, and decision-making under uncertainty. This skill empowers strategic thinking through scenario construction, probabilistic analysis, and the creation of robust planning frameworks that prepare organisations for multiple potential futures whilst enabling adaptive strategy development.

## Core Capabilities

### Scenario Construction and Development
- **Scenario Framework Design**: Create comprehensive scenario frameworks including scenario types, development methodologies, and analytical structures
- **Future State Visualisation**: Develop detailed future state scenarios including narrative development, quantitative projections, and multi-dimensional scenario characterisation
- **Scenario Logic and Consistency**: Ensure scenario logical consistency including causal relationships, internal coherence, and plausible development pathways

### Probabilistic Analysis and Quantification
- **Scenario Probability Assessment**: Assign probability estimates including subjective probability assessment, expert judgment aggregation, and uncertainty quantification
- **Monte Carlo Simulation and Modelling**: Implement Monte Carlo methods including random variable simulation, outcome distribution analysis, and sensitivity testing
- **Decision Tree and Pathway Analysis**: Create decision tree models including branching scenario analysis, conditional probability assessment, and outcome optimisation

### Strategic Planning and Decision Support
- **Scenario-Based Strategic Planning**: Integrate scenarios into strategic planning including multi-scenario strategy development, robust strategy design, and adaptive planning frameworks
- **Risk Assessment and Stress Testing**: Use scenarios for risk assessment including stress testing, worst-case planning, and resilience evaluation
- **Decision Analysis and Option Evaluation**: Support decision-making including scenario-based option evaluation, real options analysis, and adaptive decision frameworks

## Implementation Framework

### Scenario Modelling Development Process

**Phase 1: Scenario Framework and Context Analysis (120 minutes)**
- Conduct contextual analysis and scope definition including key drivers identification, stakeholder perspectives, and uncertainty mapping
- Design scenario framework including scenario categories, time horizons, and analytical dimensions
- Identify driving forces and variables including key uncertainties, critical factors, and influential trends
- Create scenario development methodology including construction process, validation criteria, and update mechanisms

**Phase 2: Scenario Construction and Quantitative Analysis (150 minutes)**
- Develop comprehensive scenario narratives including storyline construction, causal chain development, and detailed scenario descriptions
- Implement quantitative scenario modelling including variable relationships, mathematical models, and simulation development
- Conduct probability assessment including expert elicitation, historical analysis, and uncertainty quantification
- Create scenario analysis tools including sensitivity analysis, robustness testing, and scenario comparison frameworks

**Phase 3: Application and Strategic Integration (90 minutes)**
- Integrate scenarios into strategic planning including strategy development, option evaluation, and performance assessment
- Develop decision support systems including scenario dashboards, decision trees, and recommendation engines
- Create monitoring and updating systems including indicator tracking, scenario revision, and adaptive scenario management
- Establish scenario communication including visualisation tools, stakeholder engagement, and scenario storytelling
