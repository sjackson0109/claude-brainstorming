# Biomimetic Strategy - Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose
Apply principles and patterns from biological systems to strategic planning and organizational design, leveraging millions of years of evolutionary optimisation to solve complex business challenges.

## Core Capabilities

### Natural System Analysis
- **Ecosystem Mapping**: Study biological ecosystems for organizational and market insights
- **Evolutionary Principles**: Apply natural selection, adaptation, and survival mechanisms to strategy
- **Behavioral Patterns**: analyse animal behaviour for team dynamics and leadership models
- **System Resilience**: Learn from nature's approach to stability, recovery, and growth

- **Symbiotic Relationships**: Develop mutually beneficial business partnerships
