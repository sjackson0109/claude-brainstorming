# Bayesian Strategy Updating - Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose
Develop systematic approaches to update strategic beliefs and decisions using Bayesian reasoning, enabling organisations to continuously refine strategies based on new evidence and changing probabilities.

## Core Capabilities

### Bayesian Framework Integration
- **Prior Belief Formation**: Establish initial strategic assumptions based on available information
- **Evidence Integration**: Systematically incorporate new data to update strategic beliefs
- **Posterior Probability Calculation**: Determine revised probabilities after considering new evidence
- **Decision Theory**: Make optimal strategic decisions under uncertainty using updated probabilities

### Strategic Belief Systems

#### Hypothesis Formation
```
Strategic Hypothesis Framework:
- Null Hypothesis (H0): Current strategy is optimal
- Alternative Hypothesis (H1): Alternative strategy is better
- Prior Probability P(H1): Initial belief in alternative
- Likelihood P(Evidence|H1): Probability of observing evidence given hypothesis
- Posterior P(H1|Evidence): Updated belief after evidence
```

### Mathematical Foundation

#### Bayes' Theorem Application
```
P(Strategy Success | New Evidence) = 
    P(New Evidence | Strategy Success) × P(Strategy Success) 
    ÷ P(New Evidence)

Where:
- P(Strategy Success): Prior probability of strategy working
- P(New Evidence | Strategy Success): Likelihood of evidence given success
- P(New Evidence): Total probability of observing the evidence
```
