# Product Roadmap Planning Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Product Roadmap Planning Brainstorming Skill specialises in strategic product development planning, feature prioritisation, and systematic roadmap creation that aligns product evolution with business objectives and market needs. This skill empowers strategic thinking through comprehensive roadmap frameworks, stakeholder alignment processes, and the creation of adaptive product strategies that balance innovation, customer value, and technical feasibility across multiple time horizons.

## Core Capabilities

### Strategic Product Vision and Goal Setting
- **Product Vision and Strategy Alignment**: Create compelling product vision statements and strategic objectives aligned with overall business strategy and market opportunities
- **Customer Need and Market Analysis**: Conduct comprehensive customer research and market analysis identifying unmet needs and product opportunities
- **Competitive Landscape and Positioning**: Analyse competitive landscape and product positioning identifying differentiation opportunities and competitive advantages

### Feature Prioritisation and Roadmap Development
- **Feature Prioritisation Frameworks**: Implement systematic prioritisation frameworks including value vs effort analysis, user impact assessment, and strategic alignment scoring
- **Timeline and Milestone Planning**: Create realistic timeline planning including development milestones, release planning, and dependency management
- **Resource Allocation and Capacity Planning**: Plan resource allocation including development capacity, budget requirements, and skill availability

### Stakeholder Alignment and Communication
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Facilitate collaboration between product, engineering, design, marketing, and sales teams ensuring alignment and shared understanding
- **Stakeholder Communication and Buy-in**: Create compelling roadmap presentations and communication strategies ensuring stakeholder alignment and support
- **Feedback Integration and Iteration**: Implement systematic feedback collection and roadmap iteration processes ensuring continuous improvement and adaptation

## Implementation Framework

### Product Roadmap Planning Process

**Phase 1: Strategic Foundation and Vision Development (120 minutes)**
- Define product vision and strategic objectives including mission statement, success metrics, and long-term goals
- Conduct market research and customer analysis including user interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis
- Assess current product state including feature inventory, performance analysis, and user feedback evaluation
- Establish roadmap scope and time horizons including short-term, medium-term, and long-term planning periods

**Phase 2: Feature Analysis and Prioritisation (150 minutes)**
- Generate comprehensive feature backlog including user stories, feature descriptions, and acceptance criteria
- Conduct feature prioritisation using multiple frameworks including business value, user impact, technical complexity, and strategic alignment
- Perform feasibility analysis including technical assessment, resource requirements, and dependency mapping
- Create feature clustering and theme development grouping related features into coherent product initiatives

**Phase 3: Roadmap Creation and Stakeholder Alignment (90 minutes)**
- Design roadmap visualisation including timeline presentation, milestone mapping, and progress tracking mechanisms
- Facilitate stakeholder review sessions including feedback collection, alignment discussions, and commitment securing
- Create roadmap communication materials including executive summaries, team briefings, and customer communications
- Establish roadmap governance including review cycles, update processes, and success measurement criteria
