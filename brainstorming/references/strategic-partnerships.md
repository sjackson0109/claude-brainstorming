# Strategic Partnerships Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Strategic Partnerships Brainstorming Skill specialises in identifying, developing, and managing strategic alliances and collaborative relationships that drive mutual value creation, competitive advantage, and sustainable growth. This skill empowers strategic thinking through partnership strategy, alliance design, and the creation of comprehensive collaboration frameworks that leverage complementary strengths whilst achieving shared objectives and market success.

## Core Capabilities

### Partnership Strategy and Opportunity Identification
- **Strategic Partnership Assessment**: Evaluate partnership opportunities including strategic fit analysis, value creation potential, and competitive advantage assessment
- **Partnership Ecosystem Mapping**: Map comprehensive partnership ecosystems including value chain partners, technology collaborators, and market access facilitators
- **Collaboration Model Design**: Design partnership structures including joint ventures, strategic alliances, licensing agreements, and collaborative frameworks

### Partner Selection and Due Diligence
- **Partner Evaluation and Selection**: Conduct comprehensive partner assessment including capability evaluation, cultural alignment, and strategic compatibility analysis
- **Due Diligence and Risk Assessment**: Perform partnership due diligence including financial assessment, legal review, and risk evaluation
- **Negotiation Strategy and Terms Development**: Develop partnership negotiations including value sharing mechanisms, governance structures, and performance frameworks

### Partnership Management and Value Optimisation
- **Alliance Governance and Management**: Implement partnership governance including joint steering committees, operational coordination, and performance monitoring
- **Value Creation and Optimisation**: Maximise partnership value including synergy realisation, capability enhancement, and market opportunity exploitation
- **Relationship Management and Evolution**: Manage partnership evolution including relationship development, conflict resolution, and strategic adaptation

## Implementation Framework

### Strategic Partnership Development Process

**Phase 1: Partnership Strategy and Opportunity Analysis (120 minutes)**
- Conduct strategic partnership assessment including business strategy alignment, partnership rationale, and value creation opportunity identification
- Develop partnership strategy framework including partnership types, target partner profiles, and collaboration models
- Implement ecosystem mapping including potential partner identification, competitive analysis, and market opportunity assessment
- Create partnership business case including value proposition development, investment requirements, and expected return analysis

**Phase 2: Partner Evaluation and Alliance Structuring (150 minutes)**
- Execute comprehensive partner evaluation including capability assessment, cultural fit analysis, and strategic alignment evaluation
- Conduct partnership due diligence including financial review, legal assessment, and operational compatibility analysis
- Develop alliance structure including governance models, operational frameworks, and performance measurement systems
- Create partnership agreements including legal frameworks, intellectual property arrangements, and risk allocation mechanisms

**Phase 3: Partnership Launch and Management Optimisation (90 minutes)**
- Implement partnership launch including integration planning, team formation, and operational coordination establishment
- Establish performance monitoring including KPI development, success measurement, and continuous improvement processes
- Create partnership governance including joint management committees, escalation procedures, and strategic review cycles
- Design relationship enhancement including communication protocols, conflict resolution mechanisms, and partnership evolution planning
