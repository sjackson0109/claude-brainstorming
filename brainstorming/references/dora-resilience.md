# DORA Operational Resilience Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act) Operational Resilience Brainstorming Skill specialises in comprehensive ICT risk management, operational resilience framework implementation, and digital operational continuity. This skill empowers strategic thinking through systematic digital risk assessment, third-party risk management, and the development of robust operational resilience capabilities that ensure business continuity in the face of digital and cyber threats.

## Core Capabilities

### ICT Risk Management Framework
- **Comprehensive ICT Risk Assessment**: Systematically identify, assess, and manage information and communication technology risks across all business operations and services
- **Risk-Based ICT Governance**: Implement ICT risk management governance frameworks that align with business strategy and regulatory requirements
- **ICT Risk Monitoring and Reporting**: Create continuous monitoring systems for ICT risks with comprehensive reporting to senior management and regulators

### Operational Resilience Strategy Development
- **Business Continuity and ICT**: Design business continuity frameworks that specifically address ICT service dependencies and digital operational requirements
- **Critical Service Identification**: Systematically identify and classify critical and important functions dependent on ICT services
- **Recovery and Continuity Planning**: Develop comprehensive recovery strategies ensuring continuity of critical business functions during ICT disruptions

### Third-Party ICT Risk Management
- **ICT Third-Party Risk Assessment**: Implement comprehensive risk assessment processes for ICT service providers and technology vendors
- **Supplier Concentration Risk Management**: Manage concentration risks arising from dependencies on critical ICT third-party service providers
- **Contract and SLA Management**: Create robust contractual frameworks with ICT service providers including service level agreements and risk mitigation requirements

## Implementation Framework

### DORA Compliance Development Process

**Phase 1: ICT Risk Assessment and Current State Analysis (90 minutes)**
- Conduct comprehensive mapping of ICT systems, services, and dependencies supporting critical business functions
- Assess current ICT risk management capabilities against DORA requirements and industry best practices
- Identify critical ICT third-party services and assess concentration risks and systemic dependencies
- Evaluate existing business continuity and disaster recovery capabilities for ICT dependencies

**Phase 2: Operational Resilience Strategy Design (120 minutes)**
- Design comprehensive ICT governance framework including risk management, third-party management, and incident management
- Create operational resilience strategy addressing ICT service continuity and recovery requirements
- Develop ICT risk appetite framework and risk tolerance levels aligned with business objectives
- Design monitoring and testing frameworks for ongoing operational resilience validation

**Phase 3: Implementation and Compliance Validation (90 minutes)**
- Implement ICT risk management controls and operational resilience capabilities according to DORA requirements
- Establish ongoing monitoring, testing, and reporting systems for operational resilience and regulatory compliance
- Create incident response and crisis management capabilities for ICT disruptions and cyber incidents
- Develop continuous improvement processes for operational resilience enhancement based on testing and experience

### DORA Pillar Integration Framework

**ICT Risk Management (Pillar 1)**
- **ICT Risk Identification and Assessment**: Systematic identification and assessment of ICT risks including cyber risks, operational risks, and technology risks
- **ICT Risk Treatment and Mitigation**: Implementation of risk treatment strategies including preventive controls, detective controls, and corrective measures
- **ICT Risk Monitoring and Review**: Continuous monitoring of ICT risk exposure and effectiveness of risk treatment measures

**ICT-Related Incident Management (Pillar 2)**
- **Incident Detection and Classification**: Rapid detection and appropriate classification of ICT-related incidents including cyber attacks and system failures
- **Incident Response and Communication**: Systematic incident response including internal coordination and regulatory notification requirements
- **Incident Recovery and Learning**: Comprehensive incident recovery and post-incident learning for resilience improvement

**Digital Operational Resilience Testing (Pillar 3)**
- **Advanced Testing Programmes**: Implementation of sophisticated testing including penetration testing and red team exercises
- **Scenario-Based Testing**: Testing based on severe but plausible scenarios including cyber attacks and system failures
- **Third-Party Testing Coordination**: Coordination of testing activities involving critical ICT third-party service providers
