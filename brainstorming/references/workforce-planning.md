# Workforce Planning Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Workforce Planning Brainstorming Skill specialises in systematic analysis and strategic planning of human capital requirements to ensure organisational capability alignment with business objectives. This skill empowers strategic thinking through talent forecasting, competency gap analysis, and the creation of comprehensive workforce strategies that optimise human resources whilst building sustainable competitive advantage through people capabilities.

## Core Capabilities

### Workforce Analysis and Demand Forecasting
- **Workforce Demographics and Analytics**: Analyse current workforce including demographic analysis, skills inventory, performance assessment, and retention patterns
- **Business-Driven Workforce Demand**: Forecast future workforce needs including business strategy alignment, growth planning, and capability requirement analysis
- **Skills Gap Analysis and Competency Mapping**: Identify competency gaps including current vs. required skills analysis, capability assessment, and development need identification

### Strategic Talent Planning and Acquisition
- **Talent Acquisition Strategy and Pipeline Development**: Design recruitment strategies including talent sourcing, employer branding, and pipeline development
- **Succession Planning and Leadership Development**: Create succession frameworks including leadership pipeline development, critical role planning, and knowledge transfer
- **Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Planning**: Implement DEI workforce strategies including diverse talent acquisition, inclusive culture development, and equity planning

### Workforce Development and Optimisation
- **Learning and Development Strategy**: Design capability development including skills training, leadership development, and continuous learning programs
- **Performance Management and Career Planning**: Implement performance systems including goal alignment, career pathing, and talent development
- **Workforce Flexibility and Future of Work**: Design flexible workforce models including remote work planning, gig economy integration, and hybrid workforce strategies

## Implementation Framework

### Workforce Planning Development Process

**Phase 1: Current State Analysis and Future Demand Assessment (120 minutes)**
- Conduct comprehensive workforce analysis including current capability assessment, demographic analysis, and performance evaluation
- Analyse business strategy and growth plans including strategic objectives, expansion plans, and capability requirements
- Assess external labour market including talent availability, competitive analysis, and industry trend evaluation
- Create workforce demand forecasting including quantitative projections, skill requirement analysis, and timing assessment

**Phase 2: Gap Analysis and Strategic Planning (150 minutes)**
- Perform detailed gap analysis including skills gaps, capacity gaps, and capability shortfalls identification
- Develop workforce strategy including talent acquisition planning, development strategies, and retention initiatives
- Design organisational structure including role design, reporting relationships, and team formation
- Create succession planning including leadership development, critical role coverage, and knowledge management

**Phase 3: Implementation and Performance Management (90 minutes)**
- Implement workforce plans including recruitment execution, development program launch, and performance system establishment
- Design workforce analytics including performance measurement, predictive analytics, and workforce dashboard development
- Create change management including communication strategies, stakeholder engagement, and culture transformation
- Establish continuous improvement including regular workforce reviews, strategy refinement, and adaptive planning
