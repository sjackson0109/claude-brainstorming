# Technology Roadmapping Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Technology Roadmapping Brainstorming Skill specialises in systematic technology planning, innovation pathway development, and strategic technology investment decisions that align technological capabilities with business objectives. This skill empowers strategic thinking through technology foresighting, roadmap development, and the creation of comprehensive frameworks that guide technology evolution whilst ensuring competitive advantage and sustainable innovation success.

## Core Capabilities

### Technology Assessment and Horizon Scanning
- **Technology Landscape Analysis**: Conduct comprehensive technology environment assessment including emerging technology identification, maturity evaluation, and competitive technology intelligence
- **Innovation Trend Analysis and Forecasting**: Implement trend analysis including technology trajectory forecasting, hype cycle assessment, and innovation pattern recognition
- **Technology Readiness and Maturity Assessment**: Evaluate technology readiness including TRL (Technology Readiness Level) assessment, commercialisation timeline, and adoption barrier analysis

### Strategic Technology Planning and Roadmap Development
- **Multi-Layered Technology Roadmapping**: Develop comprehensive roadmaps including market layer, product layer, technology layer, and research layer integration
- **Technology-Business Alignment**: Align technology development with business strategy including market opportunity mapping, customer needs analysis, and value proposition development
- **Investment Prioritisation and Portfolio Management**: Prioritise technology investments including portfolio balancing, risk assessment, and resource allocation optimisation

### Innovation Ecosystem and Partnership Strategy
- **Technology Partnership and Collaboration**: Design technology partnerships including R&D collaboration, technology licensing, and innovation ecosystem development
- **Open Innovation and External Integration**: Implement open innovation including external technology acquisition, startup collaboration, and university partnerships
- **Intellectual Property and Technology Transfer**: Manage IP strategy including patent portfolio development, technology transfer, and intellectual property commercialisation

## Implementation Framework

### Technology Roadmapping Development Process

**Phase 1: Technology Intelligence and Market Analysis (120 minutes)**
- Conduct technology landscape scanning including current state assessment, competitive intelligence, and emerging technology identification
- Perform market and customer analysis including market opportunity assessment, customer need identification, and value proposition development
- Execute competitive technology analysis including competitor roadmap analysis, patent landscape review, and competitive advantage assessment
- Create technology assessment framework including evaluation criteria, maturity models, and technology selection methodology

**Phase 2: Roadmap Construction and Strategic Integration (150 minutes)**
- Develop multi-layer technology roadmaps including market evolution, product development, technology development, and research requirements
- Create technology-business alignment including strategic objective mapping, business case development, and value creation analysis
- Implement scenario planning including multiple technology pathways, contingency planning, and adaptive roadmap development
- Design investment strategy including budget allocation, risk management, and portfolio optimisation

**Phase 3: Implementation Planning and Ecosystem Orchestration (90 minutes)**
- Create implementation framework including project planning, milestone development, and resource allocation strategies
- Establish innovation ecosystem including partnership development, collaboration frameworks, and external relationship management
- Design monitoring and updating systems including progress tracking, roadmap revision, and adaptive strategy management
- Develop communication and stakeholder engagement including roadmap communication, stakeholder alignment, and executive reporting
