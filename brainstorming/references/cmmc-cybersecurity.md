# CMMC Cybersecurity - Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose
Develop comprehensive strategies for implementing Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) compliance across organisations, integrating security requirements with business strategy and operational excellence.

## Core Capabilities

### CMMC Framework Understanding
- **Maturity Levels**: Comprehensive knowledge of CMMC Levels 1-3 requirements and progressions
- **Domain Integration**: Security across 17 capability domains including access control, incident response, and risk management  
- **Practice Implementation**: Systematic implementation of security practices and processes
- **Assessment Preparation**: Readiness for third-party CMMC assessments and certifications

### CMMC Level Structure

#### Level 1: Basic Cyber Hygiene
```
Foundational Requirements:
- 17 Security Practices
- Safeguard Federal Contract Information (FCI)
- Basic safeguarding requirements
- Annual self-assessment
- Focus: Protection of FCI
```

#### Level 2: Intermediate Cyber Hygiene  
- 110 Security Practices across all domains
- Protect Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)
- Implementation of NIST SP 800-171 controls
- Triennial third-party assessment
- Enhanced documentation and process maturity

#### Level 3: Advanced/Progressive
- 110+ Advanced practices
- Protection against Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)
- Additional enhanced security practices
- Expert-level assessment requirements
- Sophisticated threat protection capabilities
