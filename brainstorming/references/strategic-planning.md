# Strategic Planning Brainstorming

## Activation Triggers

This skill responds to strategic planning brainstorming requests:

- "Brainstorm market entry strategy for [new market]"
- "Help me brainstorm expansion strategy for [business]"
- "Let's brainstorm competitive positioning against [competitors]"
- "Brainstorm long-term business strategy for [industry]"
- "Help me brainstorm strategic direction for [company]"
- "Let's brainstorm partnership strategy for [growth]"
- "Brainstorm market opportunities for [business model]"
- "Help me brainstorm strategic priorities for [timeframe]"

## Scope

**What this covers:**
- Long-term strategic direction and vision setting
- Market entry and expansion strategies
- Competitive positioning and advantage development
- Strategic partnerships and alliances
- Business model evolution and innovation
- High-level resource allocation and priority setting
- Strategic risk assessment and scenario planning

**What this doesn't cover:**
- Detailed product features (use Product Development Brainstorming)
- Technical implementation details (use Technical Architecture Brainstorming)
- Day-to-day operations (use Business Operations Brainstorming)
- Tactical marketing execution (use Commercial Strategy Brainstorming)

## Conversation Rules

1. **One question at a time** - Focus on specific strategic decisions
2. **Multiple choice preferred** - Present strategic options for easier evaluation
3. **Lead with recommendation** - Propose 2-3 approaches, recommend one with reasoning
4. **Long-term focused** - Think beyond immediate tactical needs
5. **Validate incrementally** - Present strategic sections, check alignment with vision

## Process

**Understanding the strategic context:**
- Current market position and competitive landscape
- Business goals and long-term vision
- Available resources and core capabilities
- Market trends and future opportunities

**Exploring strategic options:**
- Propose 2-3 different strategic directions with trade-offs
- Consider market dynamics and competitive responses
- Evaluate resource requirements and risk levels

**Presenting the strategy:**
- Break into logical strategic phases and milestones
- Focus on sustainable competitive advantage
- Validate strategic alignment after each section

## Key Frameworks

**Strategic Planning Questions:**
- "What's your long-term vision and where do you want to be in 3-5 years?"
- "What are your core competitive advantages and unique capabilities?"
- "What market trends and opportunities are you seeing in your industry?"
- "What strategic risks and challenges do you need to address?"

**Strategic Analysis:**
- Market opportunity assessment and sizing
- Competitive landscape analysis and positioning
- Core capability and resource evaluation
- Strategic risk and scenario planning

**Output Focus:**
- Clear strategic vision and long-term direction
- Practical strategic initiatives with priorities
- Sustainable competitive positioning
- Strategic roadmap with measurable milestones
