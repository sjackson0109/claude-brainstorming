# Technical Architecture Brainstorming

## Activation Triggers

This skill responds to technical architecture brainstorming requests:

- "Brainstorm system architecture for [application]"
- "Help me brainstorm cloud strategy for [system]"
- "Let's brainstorm integration approach for [services]"
- "Brainstorm DevOps pipeline for [deployment]"
- "Help me brainstorm scalability strategy for [platform]"
- "Let's brainstorm infrastructure design for [requirements]"
- "Brainstorm database design for [data needs]"
- "Help me brainstorm security architecture"

## Scope

**What this covers:**
- System architecture patterns and component design
- Cloud platform selection and infrastructure planning
- Integration strategies and API design
- DevOps pipeline and deployment automation
- Scalability and performance optimisation
- Database design and data architecture
- Security architecture and compliance requirements

**What this doesn't cover:**
- Product features and user experience (use Product Development Brainstorming)
- Business strategy and market positioning (use Commercial Strategy Brainstorming)
- Team processes and workflows (use Business Operations Brainstorming)
- High-level business direction (use Strategic Planning Brainstorming)

## Conversation Rules

1. **One question at a time** - Focus on specific technical decisions
2. **Multiple choice preferred** - Present technical options for easier evaluation
3. **Lead with recommendation** - Propose 2-3 approaches, recommend one with reasoning
4. **Simplicity first** - Choose the simplest solution that meets requirements
5. **Validate incrementally** - Present architecture sections, check technical alignment

## Process

**Understanding the technical requirements:**
- Current system state and constraints
- Performance, scalability, and reliability requirements
- Security, compliance, and operational needs
- Budget, timeline, and team capabilities

**Exploring architecture options:**
- Propose 2-3 different approaches with clear trade-offs
- Consider complexity, maintainability, and team expertise
- Evaluate technology choices and integration requirements

**Presenting the design:**
- Break into logical components with clear interfaces
- Focus on scalable, maintainable solutions
- Validate technical feasibility after each section

## Key Frameworks

**Architecture Planning Questions:**
- "What are your performance and scalability requirements?"
- "What's your team's current technical expertise and preferred technologies?"
- "What are your security, compliance, and operational requirements?"
- "What's your budget and timeline for implementation?"

**Technical Decisions:**
- Architecture patterns (microservices vs monolith, serverless vs containers)
- Cloud platform selection (AWS, Azure, GCP) and service choices
- Database design (SQL vs NoSQL, managed vs self-hosted)
- Integration patterns (REST APIs, message queues, event-driven)

**Output Focus:**
- Clear system architecture with component relationships
- Practical technology choices with rationale
- Scalable and maintainable design approach
- Implementation plan with risk mitigation
