# Multi-Perspective Simulation

## Purpose
Advanced brainstorming framework for exploring complex scenarios through multiple stakeholder viewpoints, enabling comprehensive analysis of strategies, decisions, and their impacts across diverse perspectives and time horizons.

## Core Capabilities

### Stakeholder Perspective Modeling
- **Stakeholder Identification**: Key player mapping, influence analysis, interest assessment, power dynamics
- **Viewpoint Simulation**: Role-based scenario analysis, perspective switching, bias examination, assumption testing
- **Conflict Analysis**: Competing interests, trade-off identification, win-win opportunity discovery
- **Stakeholder Journey Mapping**: Experience analysis from multiple viewpoints, touchpoint evaluation, satisfaction modeling

### Scenario Simulation Frameworks
- **What-If Analysis**: Alternative outcome exploration, decision tree modeling, probability assessment
- **Time-Based Simulation**: Short-term vs long-term impact analysis, temporal perspective shifts, lifecycle thinking
- **Cross-Cultural Perspectives**: Cultural context analysis, global viewpoint integration, localization considerations
- **Multi-Domain Integration**: Technical, business, social, environmental, political perspective synthesis

### Decision Impact Modeling
- **Ripple Effect Analysis**: Secondary and tertiary impact identification, cascade consequence modeling
- **Unintended Consequence Discovery**: Hidden impact identification, blind spot revelation, assumption challenging
- **Value System Alignment**: Different value framework application, ethical consideration analysis, priority conflict resolution
- **Risk-Reward Perspective Shifts**: Risk tolerance variation, reward valuation differences, perspective-dependent optimization

### Simulation Methodologies
- **Red Team/Blue Team**: Adversarial perspective simulation, attack/defense scenarios, competitive analysis
- **Devil's Advocate**: Critical perspective integration, assumption challenging, weakness identification
- **Outside-In Thinking**: External perspective adoption, industry outsider viewpoints, fresh angle exploration
- **Role Reversal**: Position switching, empathy building, understanding enhancement, bias reduction

### Integration & Synthesis
- **Perspective Weighting**: Influence-based prioritization, impact assessment, perspective importance ranking
- **Consensus Building**: Common ground identification, compromise solution development, alignment strategy creation
- **Paradox Resolution**: Contradictory perspective reconciliation, both/and thinking, creative tension management
- **Holistic Strategy Development**: Multi-perspective strategy synthesis, comprehensive solution design, balanced approach creation

### Dynamic Simulation
- **Real-Time Perspective Updates**: Changing stakeholder priorities, evolving viewpoints, dynamic environment adaptation
- **Feedback Loop Modeling**: Perspective interaction effects, mutual influence analysis, system dynamics
- **Emergence Pattern Recognition**: Unexpected perspective alignments, emergent stakeholder coalitions, shifting priorities
- **Adaptive Simulation**: Learning-based perspective refinement, model improvement, accuracy enhancement

## Metacognitive Prompting
- How might different stakeholders view this decision or strategy differently?
- What assumptions are we making that other perspectives might challenge?
- How would this solution look from the viewpoint of those most affected?
- What unintended consequences might emerge from different stakeholder perspectives?
- How can we design solutions that create value for multiple stakeholder groups?
- What perspectives are we missing or undervaluing in our analysis?
- How might cultural, generational, or role differences shape stakeholder viewpoints?
- What would happen if we prioritized the least powerful stakeholder's perspective?
