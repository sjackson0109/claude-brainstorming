# Change Readiness Assessment Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Change Readiness Assessment Brainstorming Skill specialises in evaluating organisational capability and preparedness for implementing strategic changes, transformations, and new initiatives. This skill empowers strategic thinking through readiness analysis, change impact evaluation, and the creation of comprehensive frameworks that identify change enablers and barriers whilst ensuring successful change implementation and sustainable adoption.

## Core Capabilities

### Organisational Readiness Analysis and Culture Assessment
- **Cultural Analysis and Change Receptivity**: Assess organisational culture including change tolerance, adaptability levels, and cultural readiness factors
- **Leadership Readiness and Change Capability**: Evaluate leadership commitment including change sponsorship, leadership alignment, and change leadership capability
- **Employee Engagement and Change Attitude**: Analyse employee readiness including change attitude assessment, engagement levels, and resistance identification

### Change Impact Assessment and Risk Evaluation
- **Change Impact Analysis and Stakeholder Assessment**: Identify change impacts including stakeholder impact assessment, process implications, and organisational effect evaluation
- **Risk Assessment and Barrier Identification**: Evaluate change risks including implementation barriers, resistance sources, and potential failure points
- **Resource and Capability Gap Analysis**: Assess resource requirements including skill gaps, capacity limitations, and capability development needs

### Readiness Enhancement and Change Preparation
- **Change Strategy and Communication Planning**: Develop change strategies including communication approaches, stakeholder engagement, and change messaging
- **Training and Development Need Assessment**: Identify development requirements including skill enhancement, training needs, and capability building programs
- **Implementation Planning and Success Enablement**: Design implementation support including change management planning, success factor enhancement, and readiness improvement

## Implementation Framework

### Change Readiness Assessment Development Process

**Phase 1: Stakeholder Mapping and Current State Analysis (120 minutes)**
- Conduct stakeholder identification and mapping including change stakeholder analysis, influence assessment, and engagement requirement identification
- Assess current organisational state including culture evaluation, capability assessment, and baseline establishment
- Analyse change history and experience including previous change success, lessons learned, and change pattern identification
- Establish assessment framework including evaluation criteria, measurement approaches, and analysis methodology

**Phase 2: Comprehensive Readiness Evaluation and Analysis (150 minutes)**
- Implement readiness assessment including cultural readiness, leadership commitment, and employee preparedness evaluation
- Conduct change impact analysis including process impact, stakeholder effect, and organisational implications
- Identify readiness gaps including capability gaps, resistance areas, and preparation requirements
- Assess change complexity including scope analysis, difficulty evaluation, and implementation challenge identification

**Phase 3: Readiness Enhancement and Implementation Planning (90 minutes)**
- Develop readiness improvement strategies including capability building, resistance management, and engagement enhancement
- Create change preparation plans including training programs, communication strategies, and stakeholder engagement
- Design implementation support including change management framework, success enablers, and risk mitigation
- Establish monitoring and tracking including readiness measurement, progress tracking, and adaptation mechanisms
