# Commercial Strategy Brainstorming

## Activation Triggers

This skill responds to commercial strategy brainstorming requests:

- "Brainstorm go-to-market strategy for [product/service]"
- "Help me brainstorm pricing strategy for [offering]"
- "Let's brainstorm marketing approach for [target market]"
- "Brainstorm sales process for [customer type]"
- "Help me brainstorm customer acquisition strategy"
- "Let's brainstorm positioning against [competitors]"
- "Brainstorm revenue model for [business]"
- "Help me brainstorm brand strategy"

## Scope

**What this covers:**
- Go-to-market planning and launch strategies
- Pricing models and packaging approaches
- Sales process design and customer acquisition funnels
- Marketing strategies and campaign planning
- Brand positioning and competitive differentiation
- Customer segmentation and targeting
- Revenue model design and optimisation

**What this doesn't cover:**
- Product roadmaps or feature planning (use Product Development Brainstorming)
- Technical implementation (use Technical Architecture Brainstorming)
- Internal processes (use Business Operations Brainstorming)
- Long-term strategic planning (use Strategic Planning Brainstorming)

## Conversation Rules

1. **One question at a time** - Don't overwhelm with multiple questions
2. **Multiple choice preferred** - Offer options when possible for easier responses
3. **Lead with recommendation** - Present 2-3 approaches, but recommend one with reasoning
4. **Customer-centric focus** - Always start with customer needs and market demand
5. **Validate incrementally** - Present strategy sections, check alignment before continuing

## Process

**Understanding the opportunity:**
- Current market position and competitive landscape
- Target customers and their pain points
- Value proposition and differentiation
- Revenue goals and success metrics

**Exploring approaches:**
- Propose 2-3 different strategies with clear trade-offs
- Lead with recommended option and explain reasoning
- Consider budget constraints and timeline requirements

**Presenting the strategy:**
- Break into sections of 200-300 words
- Focus on actionable, implementable recommendations
- Validate alignment with business objectives after each section

## Key Frameworks

**Market Research Questions:**
- "What's your total addressable market and realistic market share targets?"
- "Who are your ideal customers and what problems do they face?"
- "How do they currently solve this and what influences their decisions?"
- "Who are your main competitors and how will you differentiate?"

**Strategy Development:**
- Market entry approach (direct sales, partners, digital-first, hybrid)
- Pricing strategy (penetration, skimming, competitive, value-based)
- Channel strategy (essential vs nice-to-have channels)
- Customer acquisition funnel (awareness → interest → purchase → advocacy)

**Output Focus:**
- Clear target segments and buyer personas
- Compelling value proposition and messaging
- Practical sales and marketing tactics
- Measurable goals and success metrics
