# Yield optimisation

## Purpose
Yield optimisation provides systematic approaches to maximising output, efficiency, and profitability across various business operations by identifying and implementing strategies that increase productive capacity, reduce waste, and improve resource utilisation. This brainstorming skill enables teams to analyse performance bottlenecks, optimise resource allocation, and implement improvements that enhance overall yield while maintaining quality standards and operational sustainability.

## Core Capabilities
- **Performance Analysis and Bottleneck Identification**: Comprehensive analysis of operational performance to identify constraints, bottlenecks, and optimisation opportunities across the value chain
- **Resource Allocation and Capacity optimisation**: Strategic optimisation of resources including equipment, personnel, materials, and capital to maximise productive output and efficiency
- **Process Efficiency and Lean Improvement**: Implementation of process improvements that reduce waste, eliminate non-value-added activities, and streamline operations for higher yield
- **Quality-Yield Balance optimisation**: Balancing yield maximization with quality maintenance to ensure increased output doesn't compromise product or service standards
- **Technology Integration and Automation**: Leveraging technology and automation to improve operational efficiency and increase yield through enhanced precision and reduced variability
- **Demand-Supply optimisation**: Aligning production and service delivery capacity with demand patterns to optimise yield while avoiding overproduction or underutilization
- **Continuous Improvement and Monitoring**: Implementing ongoing monitoring and improvement systems that maintain optimal yield performance and adapt to changing conditions
- **Cost-Yield Relationship Management**: Understanding and optimising the relationship between costs and yield to maximise profitability while maintaining operational efficiency
