# Data-Driven Hypothesis Testing Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Data-Driven Hypothesis Testing Brainstorming Skill specialises in systematic hypothesis formulation, statistical testing methodology, and evidence-based decision making. This skill empowers strategic thinking through rigorous scientific methodology application, experimental design, and statistical analysis that transforms assumptions into validated insights for more effective business strategy and operational decisions.

## Core Capabilities

### Systematic Hypothesis Development Framework
- **Hypothesis Formation and Structuring**: Develop clear, testable hypotheses that translate business questions into measurable statements with defined success criteria
- **Assumption Documentation and Testing**: Systematically identify and document underlying assumptions, creating testable frameworks for assumption validation
- **Multiple Hypothesis Management**: Design frameworks for managing and testing multiple competing hypotheses simultaneously while avoiding confirmation bias

### Experimental Design and Statistical Methodology
- **Controlled Experimentation Design**: Create rigorous experimental designs including A/B testing, multivariate testing, and randomised controlled trials
- **Statistical Power and Sample Size Calculation**: Determine appropriate sample sizes and statistical power requirements for reliable hypothesis testing
- **Bias Control and Confounding Variable Management**: Implement controls for various biases and confounding variables that could affect hypothesis testing validity

### Evidence-Based Decision Making Integration
- **Statistical Significance and Practical Significance**: Balance statistical significance with business significance and practical impact assessment
- **Bayesian Inference Integration**: Apply Bayesian statistical methods for updating beliefs based on new evidence and prior knowledge
- **Decision Framework Integration**: Integrate hypothesis testing results into systematic decision-making frameworks and business strategy development

## Implementation Framework

### Data-Driven Hypothesis Testing Process

**Phase 1: Problem Definition and Hypothesis Formation (75 minutes)**
- Define clear business problems and questions requiring data-driven answers and validation
- Formulate specific, testable hypotheses with clear prediction statements and success criteria
- Identify key metrics and data sources required for hypothesis testing and validation
- Document underlying assumptions and design preliminary tests for assumption validation

**Phase 2: Experimental Design and Data Collection (105 minutes)**
- Design rigorous experimental methodology including control groups, randomisation, and bias mitigation
- Calculate required sample sizes and statistical power for reliable hypothesis testing
- Implement data collection systems ensuring data quality and experimental integrity
- Create monitoring systems for tracking experimental progress and early anomaly detection

**Phase 3: Analysis, Interpretation, and Decision Integration (75 minutes)**
- Conduct comprehensive statistical analysis including significance testing and effect size estimation
- Interpret results considering both statistical and business significance with confidence intervals
- Integrate findings into business decision-making processes and strategic planning
- Document lessons learned and create frameworks for future hypothesis testing improvement
