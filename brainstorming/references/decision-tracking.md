# Decision Tracking System Brainstorming Skill

## Purpose

The Decision Tracking System Brainstorming Skill specialises in comprehensive decision documentation, outcome monitoring, and decision-making process improvement. This skill empowers strategic thinking through systematic decision lifecycle management, decision quality assessment, and the creation of organisational learning systems that enhance future decision-making effectiveness through historical decision analysis and pattern recognition.

## Core Capabilities

### Decision Documentation and Lifecycle Management
- **Comprehensive Decision Recording**: Systematically document all significant decisions including context, assumptions, alternatives considered, and rationale
- **Decision Stakeholder Mapping**: Track all stakeholders involved in decisions including decision makers, influencers, and those affected by decisions
- **Decision Timeline and Milestone Tracking**: Monitor decision implementation progress including key milestones and critical path dependencies

### Decision Quality Assessment Framework
- **Decision Outcome Measurement**: Implement systematic measurement of decision outcomes against initial objectives and success criteria
- **Decision Process Evaluation**: Assess quality of decision-making processes including information gathering, analysis depth, and stakeholder engagement
- **Learning and Improvement Integration**: Create feedback loops that translate decision outcomes into improved decision-making capabilities and processes

### Strategic Decision Intelligence and Pattern Recognition
- **Decision Pattern Analysis**: Analyse historical decision patterns to identify successful approaches and common failure modes
- **Decision-Making Bias Identification**: Systematically identify cognitive biases and systematic errors in organisational decision-making patterns
- **Predictive Decision Analytics**: Use historical decision data to predict likely outcomes of similar future decisions and identify risk factors

## Implementation Framework

### Decision Tracking System Development Process

**Phase 1: Decision Framework and Classification (75 minutes)**
- Define decision classification system including decision types, significance levels, and tracking requirements
- Create decision documentation templates and standards ensuring comprehensive information capture
- Map decision-making processes across different organisational functions and decision types
- Establish decision stakeholder roles and responsibilities including decision makers and information providers

**Phase 2: Tracking Infrastructure and Process Implementation (105 minutes)**
- Implement decision tracking technology platforms enabling efficient decision documentation and monitoring
- Create decision workflow processes integrating tracking into natural decision-making activities
- Design decision outcome measurement frameworks with specific metrics and evaluation criteria
- Establish decision review and audit processes ensuring tracking quality and completeness

**Phase 3: Analytics and Learning System Development (90 minutes)**
- Develop decision analytics capabilities including pattern recognition and outcome prediction
- Create decision learning systems enabling extraction of insights from historical decision data
- Implement decision improvement recommendations based on analytical insights and pattern recognition
- Design decision knowledge management systems facilitating organisational learning and skill transfer
